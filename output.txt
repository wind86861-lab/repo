2.1 Clinic Management

The Clinics module is the most complex part of the system, allowing for granular control
over each medical facility.
Clinic Onboarding & Editing:
• Basic Info: Name, detailed description, and operating hours.
• Visuals: Support for high-resolution image uploads.
• Status Control: "Coming Soon" toggle for pre-launch visibility.
Operational Sub-modules:
• Location Control: Dedicated interface for managing geographic data and facilityspecific imagery.
• Package Management: Clinics can offer specific "Packages" (e.g., "Laser Surgery") with
custom pricing and discounts.
• Payment Integration: Secure configuration for Payme and Alifpay credentials per
clinic.

2.2 User & Appointment Tracking

The system provides a comprehensive view of the user base and their interactions.
• User Profiles: Captures full identity data, including Telegram usernames, phone
numbers, and gender.
• Activity Metrics: Tracks "Last Purchase" and "Purchase Count" to identify high-value
users.
• Appointment Lifecycle: Monitors the journey from booking to completion, including
payment verification.

2.3 Global Catalog & Templates

To ensure consistency across the platform, the system uses global catalogs and templates.
• Global Services: A hierarchical list of all possible medical services (e.g., "Blood
Analysis" as a parent with specific tests as children).
• Package & Checkup Templates: Standardized blueprints that can be applied to any
clinic, categorized by type (Operation, Diagnostic, etc.).

2.4 Communication & Marketing

• Banners: Time-bound promotional assets with priority-based display and redirection

URLs.

alerts, with optional integration for Telegram delivery.
